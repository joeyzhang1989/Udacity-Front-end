Panda

The panda the most well-known Chinese unique animal. This animal's main meal daily in the wild is bamboo, but it also can eat various of food. It is recognized as one of the cutest animals in the world.

  • Scientific Name: Giant panda
  • Mass: 70-120KG(Female)
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years (In the wild)
  • Height: 60-90cm(Adult)

The giant panda, also known as panda bear or simply panda, is a bear native to south central China. It is easily recognized by the large, distinctive black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body.
